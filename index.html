About Me

I have always been more interested in questions than answers.

Code allows me to build, while literature teaches me why. Between the two, I spend my time exploring human nature, loneliness, ambition, hope, and the strange beauty found in ordinary lives.

This space is not only a portfolio of projects, but also a record of the books and ideas that shaped the person behind them.
